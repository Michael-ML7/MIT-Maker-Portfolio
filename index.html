HKUST Quantum Quest - Factorisation using Quantum Approximate Optimisation Algorithm (QAOA)

Grade 11

Final phase research project under Dr. Stephen Choy and PhD student in a one-year university-level quantum computing course for ~30 gifted students.

Researched quantum factorization algorithms, comparing QAOA with Shor's algorithm. Investigated the potential of QAOA for current noisy quantum hardware and its application to breaking RSA encryption, including Variational Quantum Factoring (VQF).
